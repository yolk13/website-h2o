CAREERS WITH US

At H2O Café and Pub we have an environment that thrives towards providing the opportunities for you to grow and learn. With a very young management team; you will experience a work culture that best suits your needs and teaches you the essentials of being in the hospitality business. Hardworking and being generous are one of the core principles that our company stands for and we expect everyone associated with H2O to showcase these principles too. Working with us will enable you to join a team where communication is key and with authorised individuals at every level, you’ll learn and we’ll help you explore the highest reaches of you potential.
