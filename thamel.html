ABOUT H2O Café N Pub

Thamel is centered around Nepal’s tourist hub. It is safe to say that H2O Café and Pub, established in 2069 BS, in thamel prides itself in this culture as well. With a modern touch and an ideal location, our food speaks to you and with our team, we promise you’ll enjoy the overall ambience we have to offer! H2O Café and Pub plays its part in the travel experience Nepal has to offer by offering its unique food services and Nepalese dining experiences to tourists as well. The Pub in thamel is known for its live music as well, so drop by and enjoy!
