ABOUT H2O

H2O Café Centre started as a small café over at the owner, Sujan Shrestha’s own residence. A hobby and a knack for food, H2O Cafe has grown over a period of time expanding into new horizons with customers flocking in from all walks of life. With the success of the original café in Chabahil, H2O Café Centre expanded into Nepal’s tourist hub, thamel as H2O Café and Pub in 2069 BS. This further progressed into a brand new branch in basantapur in 2076 BS. H2O Café and Pub has continued to attract new customers and has continued serving old customers. We’re proud of our heritage and we cannot wait for you to experience the same!
